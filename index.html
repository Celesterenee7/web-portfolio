About Me

My name is Celeste and I was born in Seattle but currently reside in Bend, Oregon. I attended school at Northwest College of Art where I received my Bachelors in Fine Art. I have been working in the tech industry for the last 7 years which is ultimately what ignited my love for coding. I started building websites for the company that I currently work for and finally decided to pursue a career in software development this year. I have a friend that attended Epicodus and hearing her experience really inspired me to apply for school. Outside of coding and work, I spend a lot of time swimming and training for open water races. I still like to draw and paint when I can :)

Ultimately my goal in life is to pursue a career that allows me to be creative as a software developer!
